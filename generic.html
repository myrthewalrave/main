About Me

I am currently a first year Creative Technology student at the university of twente.
I have many interests such as programming, design and music.

I am currently studying at the university of Twente, before that I attended the highschool Porteum in Lelystad.
Porteum is at this moment the biggest highschool in the Netherlands and i enjoyed my time there. I started with Technasium, that is a project based subject that is given in some schools. I learned the basics of programming and designing there. In my third year I first took a few subjects in the year above me, but later I entirely skipped ahead. I had the subject package called NT, which is the most technical package you could choose.
In 2025 I graduated and made my choice to attend the university of Twente.

I have quite a lot of interest, but I will collaberate on my biggest ones.

Music

I've always had a passion for music and I still do. My first real experience with playing music were violin lessons, I started when I was around nine. When I was fourteen years old I stopped with violin lessons and I wanted to something else. On my fourtheenth birthday I got a Guitar for my birthday. It was just a basic and pretty cheap one, but it was enough. I started playing very often and I tought myself different songs. A year after I stopped violin lessons I started with singing lessons, which I had for two years. We also had a keyboard at home, I had bought a ukelele and a harmonica, which I all started to learn. For as of right now I have had a couple performances, a few were at school or my music school, while others were at church or other places. I still really enjoy playing and writing music and I hope that music could be incorporated in my future job.
